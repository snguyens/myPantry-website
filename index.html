myPantry

Welcome to myPantry, where you can manage your pantry, shopping lists, and recipes! You are capable of searching for new recipes based off the items contained in their pantry. If you feel the need to restock your pantry, this application will help you find nearby supermarkets that are open! This application utilizes many APIs/frameworks/libraries, including: SQLite, Recipe Puppy API, SearchUPC API, AV Foundation, Google Maps API, and Google Places API. By uniting functionalities such as list making, market finder, and recipe search, we can accomplish the goal of pantry management.

My Pantry

Welcome to your pantry! Here, you can keep track of the current items in your pantry, including expiration dates and amounts remaining. The SQLite database engine has been implemented to create the database in which the pantry items are stored and pulled. The pantry items are listed in ascending order of amount remaining.

My Recipes

Welcome to your recipes! Here, you can generate random recipes based off the ingredients available in your pantry tab. By clicking "Find me a recipe!", you are provided with a random recipe, along with the ingredients required and the website containing the recipe. The API used to pull this information is described below.




The recipes are pulled from the Recipe Puppy API. The API works by inserting comma delimited ingredients into the parameter (i.e. rice,steak,pepper) of the URL. The API then returns a list of random recipes, including the name of the recipes, the ingredients required, and the website associated with the recipe. To use this recipe searcher, you must have at least one item in your pantry. Then, items are randomly pulled from your pantry and inserted into the parameter of the API. The API will then provide a list of recipes based on these ingredients, in which one is randomly selected and provided to you.

My Shopping List

Welcome to your shopping list! Here, you can scan UPC barcode items and add the items to your shopping list. You have the option of choosing a priority (1-10) for the items in the shopping list; items with higher priority will be listed higher in your shopping list.




Barcode parsing is accomplished with the SearchUPC.com API in conjunction with Apple's AVFoundation framework. The AVFoundation framework provides services for time-based audiovisual media. The SearchUPC.com API is used to pull item names and prices from the UPC barcode numbers.

Nearby Supermarkets

Welcome to your nearby supermarkets! This application will show you the nearest supermarkets and grocery stores based on your location (within 5,000 meters). Supermarkets that are not open will not display on the map. The "Refresh" button will allow you to pull supermarkets from another region if you decide to move the map around.




The Google Places API is used to pull nearby supermarkets depending on your location. Your latitude and longitude are computed and then passed as parameters into the Google Places API. Additionally, a radius of 5,000 meters is passed into the parameter. Supermarkets within this range and still open will be displayed on the map.
